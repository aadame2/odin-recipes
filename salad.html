Salad

Refreshing salad with cucumbers, tomato, feta, onions, chick peas and olives

Ingredients

  • Arugula
  • Tomatos
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Red onion
  • Cucumber
  • Chick Peas

Steps

  • Add desired amount of Arugula base to bowl
  • chop cucumber
  • chop tomato
  • chop onions
  • add chopped ingredients to bowl
  • add chickpeas from can
  • add feta cheese
  • finally, finish off with olive oil, salt and pepper
