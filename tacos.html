Tacos

Tacos are made with varying types of meat, beans, green leaf, cheese, and taco sauce. Your choice of tomato/pico de gallo or avocado toppings.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Soyrizo
  • Black beans
  • Cheese
  • Garlic salt
  • Arugula
  • Avocado
  • Taco sauce

Steps

  • Warm up black beans in a pot
  • Cook soyrizo on medium heat
  • Warm up tortillas on stove top. Flip when browning
  • Fold tortillas in half (taco shape)
  • Add cooked soyrizo to tortilla
  • Add black beans on top of soyrizo
  • Add cheese and let melt on beans
  • Add Arugula
  • Slice avocado and add 2 slices on each taco
  • Drizzle desired amount of taco sauce
  • Sprinkle garlic salt on top

  • Enjoy!
